Laser Service

Laser Job Shop Services.    No minimum quantity required.

We provide fast turnaround laser job shop services for

  • Laser marking including  color marking on metals
  • 2D and 3D laser engraving, including micro engraving 
  • Laser etching
  • Laser annealing
  • Laser paint removing,
  • Laser drilling,
  • Laser cutting of thin sheet,
  • Laser surface texturing including laser frosting,
  • Laser surface treatment,
  • Other laser micro-processing.

    Materials can be worked on include (our laser does not work on WOOD):

  • Metals - all steels (including mold steel, stainless steel), copper (including EDM electrode), tungsten carbide, titanium, bronze, brass, anodized, chrome, aluminum, nickel, alloy;
  • Most plastics - Acrylic, ABS, PES, PVC, Polycarbonate, Lexan, Styrene and other plastics;
  • Graphite including EDM electrode and Inconel graphite;
  • Backlit plates or buttons;
  • Abrasives;
  • Ceramics;
  • Semiconductor packaging materials, IC chips, wafers, silicon;
  • PCB;
  • Other materials.
